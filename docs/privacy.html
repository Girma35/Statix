1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Gold List ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you use our mobile application Gold List (the "App"). The App is a spaced-repetition vocabulary learning tool that helps you build and retain knowledge through the Gold List distillation method.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

  1. Provide and maintain the App — Authenticate your account, store your vocabulary lists, and track your learning progress.
  2. Improve your experience — Personalize content and send study reminders (with your permission).
  3. Communicate with you — Send account-related notifications (e.g., password reset emails).
  4. Analyze aggregate usage — Understand how users interact with the App to improve features. No personal data is used for this analysis.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Where Data Is Stored

Your data is stored on Google Cloud Platform servers (Firebase) located in the United States. Local caching occurs on your device via AsyncStorage.

5.2 Security Measures

  • Encryption in transit — All data transferred between the App and Firebase is encrypted via TLS/SSL.
  • Encryption at rest — Data stored in Firestore is encrypted at rest using AES-256.
  • Secure authentication — Passwords are never stored in plaintext; Firebase Auth handles hashing and salting.
  • Access controls — Only authenticated users can access their own data via Firestore security rules.

5.3 Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the App's services. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.
